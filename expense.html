The Expenses

Here's Why You Will Enjoy Vietnam as a Tourist

Since the monthly salary in Vietnam is significantly less than an American's average monthly salary,
this makes Vietnam one of the best places to visit to enjoy an extravagant lifestyle that some could only
experience once in their lifetime. Vietnam is a very inexpensive country, especially if you are an American
and you bring USD to convert to Vietnamese currency.

  1. The majority of products cost less than half of what they would in the West.
    • You can buy a whole meal for only one dollar.
    • A Traditional Vietnamese Sandwich that is sold by street vendors may be 50 cents.
  2. The most expensive city in Vietnam is Ho Chi Minh City, which is the capital of South Vietnam,
    followed by Hanoi, which is the capital of North Vietnam.
    • A couple can easily live in the middle class in either of these two cities for less than $1,300 a month.

    and many cruises and sightseeing tours around Vietnam each day are all within reach with a monthly budget of $4,000.
